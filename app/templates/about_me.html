The road to become a Python Developer

Hello, nice to meet you!

My name is Henrique Gonzaga, Brazilian, born in Belo Horizonte, Brazil, and thank you very much for visiting my website.

I had traveled a long road until I discovered my passion for programming and became a Python programmer. I hope you can enjoy the journey I will take you through to discover my story about myself.

After finishing school, I ventured into a History degree at Universidade Federal de Ouro Preto, but after not adapting well to the course, I decided to pursue a degree in Law at Milton Campos in Belo Horizonte, and after 2 years of trying, I realized it wasn't my calling. After these two failed attempts, I decided to venture into the field of sports, as it is a passion of mine, and I also decided to move to a different country for a better future perspective, moving to Italy. There, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Sports Sciences. After those 3 years of living there, I decided to move to Portugal, new horizons, why not? As you may have noticed, I'm always seeking new challenges. In Portugal, I completed my Master's degree in Sports Sciences, and throughout my educational journey, I had various experiences as a football coach, mainly with children aged 7 to 12.
